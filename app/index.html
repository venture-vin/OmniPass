Vision

Identity is broken. Billions lack it. Yet every transaction, whether it be Basic Financial Services or Health Care, relies on Identity at the core.

Here at Omnipass we imagined how we could reinvent Identity to compliment the “Smart City” of the Future. How could we make take the advantages of Blockchain, “Security, Transparency, Immutability” and not only provide a robust verification system to serve as the foundation, but to autonomously provide the basic necessities people would need.

We are proud to announce that we will be launching our Beta in Dubai very soon.
